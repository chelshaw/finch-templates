How we put ourselves through the wringer

Our Values

Science comes first

It all comes down to trust, so we lean on rigorous research and scientific facts to provide clarity and objectivity.

More within reach

For many, living sustainably is a necessity, not a nice-to-have. We’re tired of the trendy, inaccessible lifestyle bit and aim to decouple sustainability and status.

Words have meaning

Sustainability is complicated and confusing and words like “green” and “clean” aren’t helping. We say what we mean, not just what others want to hear.

Real-life approved

We’re out to prove that sustainability doesn’t mean sacrifice. This isn’t about just being responsible on paper, it’s about showing up and having a damn good time doing it.

Progress not perfection

We’re on an ever-evolving path and no one has all the answers. We adapt and take simple, efficient steps forward because we believe our individual actions can lead to collective rewards.
